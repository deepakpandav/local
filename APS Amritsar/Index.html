ABOUT US

Empowering young minds since 1985

The Army Public School, AmritsarCantt was established on 01 May, 1985 under the directions and guidance of Mr. AK Dewan, the then General Officer in Commanding, Panther Division.The school began initially with only 156 students, which over the years have increased to 2919 students.

It was affiliated to CBSE New Delhi in the year 1985 upto 8th Standard, later upgraded to Secondary School in 1986 & Senior Secondary School in 1996. At present school is running Commerce, Humanities and Science Streams in 10+2 standard.

Principal Message
